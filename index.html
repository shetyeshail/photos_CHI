Chicago

Chicago, Illinois, USA - Summer 2019

Some photos I took during a work trip to Chicago in the summer of 2019. It was actually my first trip to Chicago and I quite enjoyed it even though I wasn't there for very long.

Photos shot on either a Ricoh 500 G 35mm film camera (I forget which film, either Kodak Portra 400 or Fujifilm Superia X-tra 400), or on a Fujifilm X-H1 with a 35mm f/2 lens. All editing was done using Darktable.
